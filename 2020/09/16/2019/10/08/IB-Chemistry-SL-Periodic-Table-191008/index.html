Chemistry SL

The Periodic Table

Unit Test I

Quote from https://quizlet.com/98728817/ib-chemistry-sl-topic-3-periodicity-flash-cards/

Question

  1. Atomic radius _____ across a period.
  2. Atomic radius _____ down a group.
  3. Generally larger than the nonmetal atoms from which they are formed
  4. Generally smaller than the metal atoms from which they are formed
  5. Electron attraction to the nucleus depends on:
  6. What’s atomic radius
  7. First ionization energy ___ across a period.
  8. First ionization energy ___ down a group.
  9. What’s first ionization energy?
  10. What’s electronegativity?

Answer

  1. Decreases
  2. Increases
  3. anions
  4. cations
  5. How many protons are in the nucleus(nuclear charge), how far the outer electrons are from the nucleus(number of shells), how many core electrons are between the outer electrons and the nucleus(shielding effect)
  6. The distance from the nucleus to the outermost electron
  7. Increases
  8. Decreases
  9. The energy to remove one outer electron from a gaseous atom
  10. A relative measure of the attraction that an atom has for a shared pair of electrons when it is covalently bonded to another atom
