Chemistry SL

The Periodic Table

Quick question

Quote from Oxford IB Chemistry Course Book (p. 77):

预测以下分子结构的键长 （predict the bond lengths )

a）iodine monobromide, 一溴化碘，IBr

b）trichloromethane 三氯甲烷（氯仿），CHCl3

Tip 1

先看一个预测分子结构键长的例子，对于卤素间化合物 interhalogen compound （BrF）：

溴bromine的原子半径= 117pm

fluorine原子半径为= 60 pm

于是 bond length of Br F = 117 + 60 = 177 pm

Tip 2

Tip 3

atomic radius of iodine = 136 pm

Answer

a)
To predict the I—Br bond length, we approximate that the atomic radius of iodine is greater than 117 pm( >atomic radius of bromine)
The bond length of IBr is approximately 117 and 136 pm , 大约是 250 pm 左右

b)
This is a polar molecule with tetrahedral molecular geometry;the C-H sigma bond has a bond length of 107 pm as the atomic radius of hydrogen and carbon are 32 and 75 pm, respectively;

With the chlorine being a much larger atom (atomic radius 100 pm), when compared to hydrogen, the bond length of the C-Cl bond is 175 pm;
