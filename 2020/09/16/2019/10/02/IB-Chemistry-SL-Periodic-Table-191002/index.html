IB Chemistry SL — Periodic Table — estimating nuclear charge (191002)

Chemistry SL

The Periodic Table

Worked example

Quote from Oxford IB Chemistry Course Book (p. 78):

Estimate the effective nuclear charge experienced by the valence electron in the alkali metal
potassium.
估算碱土金属alkali metal中的钾所相关的effective核电荷

Tips 1

Potassium, K has the electron configuration 1s22s22p63s23p64s1 and Z = 19.
K has a total of 19个质子 和 19 electrons.

Tip2

K has 19 electrons and one valence electron (it is in group 1). This means there are 18 core electrons。

另外回忆一下什么是valence electrons价电子？

valence electrons， 价电子，指原子核外电子中能与其他原子相互作用形成化学键的电子。
价电子可以决定该元素的导电性能。一般来说，原子的价电子数愈少，活性就愈高？

Tips 3

什么是nuclear charge？

nuclear charge 就是由原子中的质子产生的吸引力（为+）。在钾这个例子中，就是 the full force of attraction of the 19 protons that provide the nuclear charge
.
而valence electron（在这里是一个）因为其它18个 core electrons 核心电子部分抵消了正电荷, 因而有效核电荷大约是1：
The 18 core electrons partially cancel this positive charge and the effective nuclear charge is
approximately 1:
