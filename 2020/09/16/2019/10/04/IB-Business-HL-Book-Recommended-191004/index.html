About book

Quoted from “The Ascent of Money: A Financial History of the World”

Finance is in fact the foundation of human progress. What’s more, he reveals financial history as the essential backstory behind all history.

尼尔•弗格森（Niall Ferguson）表明，金融实际上是人类进步的基础。而且, 他揭示财务历史在所有历史的背后都是重要背景。

The evolution of credit and debt was as important as any technological innovation in the rise of civilization, from ancient Babylon to the silver mines of Bolivia

在文明的崛起中，信贷和债务的演变与任何科技的演进同等重要，从古代巴比伦到玻利维亚银矿莫不如此。

Yet the most important lesson of the financial history is that sooner or later every bubble bursts —sooner or later the bearish sellers outnumber the bullish buyers, sooner or later greed flips into fear

然而，金融学最重要的一课 历史是每个泡沫迟早都会破裂—迟早卖空的人数多于
买主看涨，迟早的贪婪变成恐惧。
