IB Chemistry SL — Stoichiometric（190915）关于水蒸气、开水温度、感觉凉爽与否等等

IB Chemistry SL

Stoichiometric relationships

Activity answers

Q1： Explain why the temperature of a boiling liquid does not increase despite energy being constantly applied

Hint 1

Intermolecular attractions

与分子间的吸引力变强或变弱有关

Hint 2

At high temperature the particles of a liquid move more quickly and the distances between the particles increase. Intermolecular attractions become less significant and eventually the liquid become gas

温度越高，分子间的吸引力就越弱，慢慢液体变气体

A1:

Energy provided to the system is being used to break down the intermolecular hydrogen bonds between the H2O molecules

Q2： Deduce which would be more painful, scalding your skin with water vapour or boiling water

粗看是开水 vs 蒸汽谁更烫的问题？但其实问题是谁的伤害更大，注意这里有一个可能认识不清的小常识错误

Hint 1

Cooling brings about the reverse processes to heating – the condensation of water vapour to form liquid water, and the freezing of liquid water to form a solid

注意，这里的凝结condensation，严格意义上是指从气体到液体。冷冻freezing， 是指从液体到固体

Hint 2

If you heat a block of ice in a beaker it will melt to form liquid water. If you continue heating the water, it will boil to form water vapour.

A2:

Water vapour results in more painful burns to the skin; boiling water and water vapour both have a temperature of 100°C; vaporisation is an endothermic process; condensation of water vapour on the skin is an exothermic process

注意，烧开的水和蒸汽都是100摄氏度。蒸汽烫伤更严重，是因为蒸汽在皮肤上凝结condensation的过程是一个放热exothermic 的过程 (与之相对的，叫吸热endothermic 的过程，在开水变成蒸汽的时候）

Q3： Explain why you might feel cold and shiver when you get out of the water at the beach on a very hot, windy day..

Hint 1

Evaporatoin

A3:

The heat from the sun and warm air passing over the body increases the rate of evaporation from the skin; evaporation is an endothermic process; water molecules leaving the surface of the skin results in a loss of energy from the skin; the response of the human body is to shiver

有意思的是太阳或周遭的热空气蒸发了皮肤上的水分，所以你会觉得凉，这个和一般认为的太阳出来就暖和，热风一吹就不冷，真还有点认识上的区别
