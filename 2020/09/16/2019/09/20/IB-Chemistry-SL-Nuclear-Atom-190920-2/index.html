Q 6

Which is correct for the following regions of theelectromagnetic spectrum?

A 6

A; the relationship between wavelength, frequency and energy is the greater the energy, the shorter (smaller) the wavelength and the higher the frequency; ultraviolet light is high energy/short wavelength when compared to visible light; infrared light is lower energy/low frequency compare to visible light;

Q 7

In the emission spectrum of hydrogen, which electronic transition would produce a line in the visible region of the electromagnetic spectrum?

A. n = 2 → n = 1

B n = 3 → n = 2

C. n = 2 → n = 3

D. n = ∞ → n = 1

A 7

B; the series of lines found in the visible region of the spectrum is called the Balmer series; they are associated with electronic transitions from upper energy levels down to the n = 2 energy level;

Tip

能看见的在n=2层

Q 8

Which describes the visible emission spectrum of hydrogen?

A. A series of lines converging at longer wavelength

B. A series of regularly spaced lines

C. A series of lines converging at lower energy

D. A series of lines converging at higher frequency

A 8

D; the line emission spectrum of hydrogen provides evidence for the existence of electron in discrete energy levels, which converge at higher energies; high energy photons of light have a higher frequency and shorter wavelength;

Q 9

What is the order of increasing energy of the orbitals within a single energy level?

A. d < s < f < p

B. s < p < d < f

C. p < s < f < d

D. f < d < p < s

A 9

B; the energy levels are split into sublevels, of which there are four common types: s,p,d and f; each has a characteristic shape and associated energy; the order of increasing energy is s<p<d<f;
