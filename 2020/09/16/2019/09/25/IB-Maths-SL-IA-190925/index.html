Again, can you come up with more Maths SL IA ideas? (190925)

Maths SL

Exploration (IA） Ideas

It’s crucial in the first two stages -the introduction/preparation stage, and the topic choice stage when students need to be guided to complete their IA.

There are some effective activities for students to do during intro/pre stage

read short articles that are examples of good writing about a mathematical topic at a suitable level for Maths SL and/or Maths HL

read past student Explorations - preferably with some information on how well the Exploration addressed the assessment criteria.
