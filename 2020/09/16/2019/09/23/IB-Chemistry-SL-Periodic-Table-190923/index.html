IB Chemistry SL (190923）—— 元素周期表一些提法和注意点

2019-10-01

Chemistry SL

The Periodic Table

Study Tips

Quote from The Periodic Table: its story and its significance (p. 10, p. 18):

Tips 1 少数元素用单一字母作为元素符号

In the modern periodic table, a small minority of elements are represented by a single letter of the alphabet. These include hydrogen氢, carbon碳, oxygen氧, nitrogen氮, sulfur硫, and fluorine氟, which appear as H, C, O, N, S, and F.

Tips 2大多数元素用两个字母作为元素符号

Most elements are depicted by two letters, the first of which is a capital letter and the second a lowercase letter. This gives rise to such element symbols as Li, Be, Ne, Ca, and Sc,for lithium锂, beryllium铍, neon氖, calcium钙, and scandium钪, respectively.

Tips 3少数新发现的元素，在其发现者还没有公开确认的情况下，由三个字母表示

For a brief period of time 曾有一个简短的时期, some heavy elements(重元素） were depicted by three letters, but many have now been given particular names, such as seaborgium for element 106. But the very recently discovered elements, such as 112, 114, and 116, whose official discoverers are still under discussion, continue to be depicted by three letters, namely, Uub, Uuq, and Uuh.

在自然界中发现的铀由三种具有相对丰度 relative abundances的同位素组成

Tips 4 传统的元素周期表由行和列组成，通过每行每列都可以观察到一些趋势

The conventional periodic table consists of rows and columns. Trends can be observed among the elements going across and down the table. Each horizontal row represents a single period of the table.

On crossing a period, one passes from metals such as potassium and calcium on the left, through transition metals such as iron, cobalt, and nickel, then through some semimetallic elements such as germanium, and on to some nonmetals such as arsenic, selenium, and bromine on the right side of the table.

In general, there is a smooth gradation in chemical and physical properties as a period is crossed, but exceptions to this general rule abound and make the study of chemistry a fascinating and unpredictably complex fi ld.

Tips 5 在元素周期表中可以看到，随着原子序数的增加，化学元素在性质上表现出近似的重复性

The periodic law states that after certain regular but varying intervals the chemical elements show an approximate repetition in their properties. For example, fluorine氟, chlorine氯, and bromine溴, share the property of forming white crystalline salts 白色晶体盐 of general formula NaX with the metal sodium钠.

**This periodic repetition of properties is the essential fact that underlies all aspects
of the periodic system.**（元素性质的周期性重复 是元素周期律所有规律的本质）

Among the elements, however, not only does the period length vary, but also the periodicity is not exact. The elements within any column of the periodic table are not exact recurrences of each other. In this respect, their periodicity is not unlike the musical scale, in which one returns to a note denoted by the same letter, which sounds like the original note but is defi nitely not identical to it, being an octave higher. （周期性像音阶，有周期性，但也不是完全重复，像是音阶升高是，音符重复出现，发音相似，写法重复，但高了八度）。
