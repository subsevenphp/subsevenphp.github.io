##Guide to the Biology IA
No matter if you are a Standard or Higher Level IB Biology student, many struggle with the broad nature of this seminal part of the IB Biology curriculum. The open-ended structure of the assignment can often overwhelm both students and teachers. Many of you may be grappling with questions like:

Where do I look for IB Biology IA ideas?
What makes an IB Biology IA a 7?
How novel does my IA idea have to be?
What IB Biology topic is easiest to write an IA on?
& Much more

I have taken note of the questions and issues my students have experienced the most trouble with during their Biology IA, in order to develop a guide based on the advice that I have provided to my students in my years of teaching. This guide will clearly answer the above questions and more while explaining how to develop a focused research question as well as the steps behind creating an experimental method before ending with advice on how to finish with a strong conclusion that meets all of the relevant IA criteria; ensuring students achieve the best possible result on what constitutes 20% of their final IB Biology grade.##
