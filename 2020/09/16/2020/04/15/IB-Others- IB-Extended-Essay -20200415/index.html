Welcome to The IB Extended Essay: Managing your research project

Quoted from https://www.futurelearn.com/courses/ib-extended-essay/8/welcome

In this course you will be shown how to complete each step of your extended essay, from choosing a topic and essay title through to planning and writing it effectively. A series of articles, videos and tasks will help you to research and write your extended essay. In addition you will develop skills you can use now, and in future studies.

This course is just one of a series designed by the University of Leeds for school-based learners. This course has been specifically designed to help those studying an International Baccalaureate (IB) curriculum at school or college, and in particular those undertaking the extended essay part of their IB Diploma Programme. As you will have seen from the welcome area, a diverse cohort have signed up.

A lot of the skills required to research and plan an extended essay are similar to those needed when studying at degree level and beyond, so we’ve designed this course to help you develop these study skills.

This map provides a quick overview of the two weeks ahead. You can also view the learning outcomes and syllabus for this course or download them from the section at the bottom of this step.
