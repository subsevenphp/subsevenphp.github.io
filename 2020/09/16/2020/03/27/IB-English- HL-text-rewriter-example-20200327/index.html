IB English HL — Text rewrite example (200327）

English HL

##Original document
**SORT OF CHINGLISH I SUPPOSE**

Rewrite Example I

The first few weeks of the semester are over, and online schooling is the only article I have read that deals with the future of technology. It’s definitely a new experience for me, but I’m glad it’s happening, even though I’ve only read about it in articles.
I keep hearing from teachers and classmates that we are living in the now and in the future and not in the past or even the present, but in the near future.
Of course I still miss seeing my classmates and playing outside together, but now everything is going smoothly and hopefully we can meet in person soon. But I am slowly getting used to my new learning environment and was sometimes not well prepared.

Rewrite Example II

The first few weeks of the semester are over, and online schooling is the only article I have read that deals with the future of technology. It’s definitely a new experience for me, but I’m glad it’s been re-read, especially now that the first few weeks are over.
I keep hearing teachers and classmates say, “We live in the now, not the future,” and I’ve always heard that from my teacher and classmates.
Of course I still miss seeing my classmates and playing outside together, but now everything is going smoothly and hopefully we can meet in person soon. But I am slowly getting used to my new learning environment and sometimes I am not well prepared.
